Our Local

We are a vibrant restaurant nestled in the heart of Cape Town. We offer a warm and welcoming atmosphere where you can savor the soul of South African cuisine. Our menu features a delicious array of traditional dishes prepared with fresh, seasonal ingredients.

Established in 2024

Our Local has become a popular venue in Cape Town’s thriving CBD, attracting local foodies and inquisitive travellers searching for the best seafood the city has to offer.

At Our Local, our core philosophy is centred around quality and sustainability. That’s why we only use fresh, locally sourced and seasonal produce in each of our dishes. As proud supporters of the South African Sustainable Seafood Initiative (SASSI), we exclusively serve fish which has been landed on South Africa shores, and never Red Listed Fish.
